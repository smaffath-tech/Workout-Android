Privacy Policy for MyChestApp

Last updated: 2025-11-29

MyChestApp ("we", "our", "us") provides a home workout planner and tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Email only if you contact us or create an account (optional). We do not require sign-in for core features.
  • Usage Data: Local workout history, exercise completion counts, device locale. Stored locally on your device (SharedPreferences).
  • Crash & Analytics (Optional): If you enable crash reporting (Crashlytics) or analytics, minimal diagnostic data and anonymized usage events may be sent to Firebase. We will ask for consent before enabling external analytics.

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and maintain app functionality (save workouts, progress, timers).
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues (with your explicit consent for crash reporting).
  • Improve features and performance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only share data with third-party providers (e.g., Firebase) if you opt-in; those providers are bound by their own privacy policies.

Storage

Data is stored locally on your device. If you enable optional cloud backup, data may be stored on Firebase / Google Cloud. You can request deletion by emailing the contact below.

Contact

If you have questions, email: support@mychestapp.example
